About Us

Stellar Detailing & Restoration is a mobile detailing company that has grown servicing the heart of Tampa. Our mission is to consistently maintain superior services by providing our full attention and expertise to bring your vehicle back to life. From the products used to perform each task to our keen eye for detail, our customers are never disappointed.

We only use top-of-the-line products to ensure the highest level of protection for your vehicle while still offering a variety of affordable services, such as seat and floor shampoo, clay bar, sealant wax, leather cleaning, paint correction, ceramic coatings and much more.

Why Take Our Services?

At Stellar Detailing & Restoration our customers’ complete satisfaction is a must, and we offer the highest standards in quality on all of our services. Any project is possible for us, providing mobile detailing services for boats, cars, trucks, RVs, campers and fleets across the greater Tampa Bay region.

Exterior Paint Correction

We offer a full range of paint correction services, starting from a wash and hand wax to complete scratch and paint correction, making your vehicle look better than brand-new.

Overspray and Tar Removal

We offer paint overspray, tree sap removal and road paint removal. Safely removing these imperfections without damaging the paint of your vehicle.

Scratch Removal

Our wet sanding and buffing techniques allow us to remove any scratch from your vehicle, leaving your paint without deep scratches and swirl-free.

Water Spot Removal

We offer complete water spot removal on all your glass, paint, and wheels. Leaving your glass polished for better visibility, and your paint and wheels shining bright.

Carpet and Upholstery

We offer hot water extraction cleaning of all carpets, seats, seatbelts, and headliners without leaving the inside of your vehicle wet or smelling like chemicals.

Odor and Mold Removal

We are capable of doing complete mold and cigarette odor removal and water damage repair using state-of-the-art ozone machines.

Leather

We offer leather cleaning and conditioning to maintain the color and texture. Preventing the leather from drying out and cracking.

Plastic

We clean all the plastic without using any dressings, including steam cleaning the vent system. Keeping your ventilation system dust- and mold-free.
